Frequently Asked Questions

Do you charge fees or commissions?

No. We’re investors, not agents. There are no listing commissions or hidden fees—our partners typically cover standard closing costs.

Will I need to make repairs or clean out the house?

No. Investors buy properties as‑is. You can leave unwanted items behind.

How fast can we close?

Closing speed depends on title, lien payoff, and your timeline. Many direct sales close in 1–3 weeks, but you are never required to move faster than you want.

Who will contact me after I submit?

A small number of our vetted, select investing partners in Florida will reach out by phone, text, and email to understand your goals and present options.

Am I obligated to sell?

No. Requesting an offer is free and there’s no obligation to accept.

Will you share my information?

We may share your information with a limited number of vetted investing partners solely to provide options. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Do you buy condos, townhomes, and mobile homes?

Yes—our partners consider single‑family, condos, townhomes, multi‑family, manufactured homes, land, and some commercial properties.
