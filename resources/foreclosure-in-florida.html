Florida Foreclosure: A Homeowner Guide

Florida uses a judicial foreclosure process. If you’ve fallen behind, you may still have options such as reinstatement, loan modification, or selling the property before the court‑ordered sale. Consider speaking with a Florida attorney or HUD‑approved housing counselor.
